DESIGN CHALLENGE 3 · PANEL B

Did effort and volume move together?

Weekly average RPE (effort) overlaid against total training volume. Error bars on RPE show the spread of effort across sessions within each week.

Key finding: Volume stabilized around 42,600 lbs/week by Week 3 and barely changed. Meanwhile RPE climbed from 7.58 → 8.65 — the same workload felt progressively harder, a potential fatigue signal.
DESIGN CHALLENGE 3 · PANEL C

When were the gains actually happening?

Each cell shows % change in estimated 1RM relative to Week 1. Hover over any cell for details. Green = gaining, red = stalled or dropped.

Key finding: Most gains were concentrated in Weeks 2–3. By Weeks 5–7 progress clearly slowed across most exercises, with the heatmap shifting from deep green to lighter shades.
